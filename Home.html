Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I track my meals?

To track your meals, click on the "Track Meals" button in the navigation bar and enter the details of your meals for the day.

Can I customize my exercise plans?

Yes, you can customize your exercise plans by clicking on the "Track Exercise" button and selecting exercises according to your preference and fitness level.

How can I set fitness goals?

To set fitness goals, navigate to the "Set Your Goals" page by clicking on the corresponding button in the navigation bar and follow the instructions to set your goals.
