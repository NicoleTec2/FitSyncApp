          Carb-Free Recipes

          Carb-Free Recipe: Avocado and Chicken Lettuce Wraps

          Ingredients:

          -1 ripe avocado

          -1 cup cooked chicken breast, shredded

          -1/4 cup diced tomatoes

          -2 tablespoons chopped cilantro

          -Juice of 1 lime

          -Salt and pepper to taste

          -Lettuce leaves for wrapping

          Instructions: In a bowl, mash the avocado with lime juice, salt, and pepper. Mix in shredded chicken, diced tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro. Spoon the avocado chicken mixture onto lettuce leaves. Roll up the lettuce leaves to form wraps. Serve and enjoy!

          Nutritional Facts (per serving): Calories: 250 Protein: 18g Carbohydrates: 10g Fat: 16g

          Sugar-Free Desserts

          Greek Yogurt Berry Parfait

          Ingredients:

          -1 cup Greek yogurt

          -1/2 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

          -1 tablespoon honey or stevia (optional)

          Instructions: In a glass or bowl, layer Greek yogurt, mixed berries, and granola. Repeat layers until the glass or bowl is filled. Drizzle honey or sprinkle stevia over the top if desired. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to eat.

          Nutritional Facts (per serving): Calories: 200 Protein: 10g Carbohydrates: 20g Fat: 8g

          High-Protein Meals

          Grilled Salmon with Asparagus

          Ingredients:

          -2 salmon fillets

          -1 bunch asparagus, trimmed

          -2 tablespoons olive oil

          -2 cloves garlic, minced

          -Salt and pepper to taste

          -Lemon wedges for serving

          Instructions: Preheat grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Brush the salmon fillets and asparagus spears with the olive oil mixture. Place salmon fillets and asparagus on the grill. Grill for 4-5 minutes per side, or until salmon is cooked through and asparagus is tender. Remove from grill and serve with lemon wedges.

          Nutritional Facts (per serving): Calories: 300 Protein: 25g Carbohydrates: 8g Fat: 20g
